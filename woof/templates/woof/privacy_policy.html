1. Who we are

Woof Dogs is an informational website dedicated to dog breeds and related content. We do not provide user accounts, registration, or login functionality.

2. Personal data you provide

In short: We only process the personal data you voluntarily provide.

The Website does not require registration and does not request names, usernames, passwords, or email addresses by default. However, if you choose to contact us through the Contact Form, we will collect the information you provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message or inquiry content

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and will not share it with third parties except where required by law.

3. Automatically collected information

When you visit the Website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device and operating system information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URLs

This information does not directly identify you and is used for analytics, security, and performance improvement.

4. Purposes of processing

  • Ensuring proper functioning of the Website
  • Analyzing usage to improve content and structure
  • Maintaining Website security and preventing abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Legal bases and regional laws

Depending on your location, your data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

European Union (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), data is processed under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), based on legitimate interests, consent (where applicable), or legal obligations.

United Kingdom (UK GDPR)

For users in the United Kingdom, data is processed in accordance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR.

United States

If you are a resident of the United States, privacy rights may apply under state laws, including:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA)
  • Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA)
  • Colorado Privacy Act (CPA)
  • Utah Consumer Privacy Act (UCPA)
  • Tennessee Information Protection Act (TIPA)

Canada

For users in Canada, personal information is handled in accordance with PIPEDA .

Australia

For users in Australia, data is processed under the Privacy Act 1988 .

Other countries

If you are located outside the regions listed above, we process information in accordance with generally accepted international data protection principles.

6. Cookies and tracking technologies

The Website uses cookies to ensure proper operation and to analyze traffic. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

More details are available in our Cookie Policy.

7. Sharing of information

We do not sell or rent personal data. Information may be shared only with analytics providers or when required by law.

8. International data transfers

Data may be processed outside your country of residence. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied.

9. Data retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

10. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data.
