Welcome to Woof Dogs!

This site offers a concise overview of dog breeds, categorized based on traits and historical purposes, as established by American Kennel Club (AKC).

Each breed group has its own unique history and purpose, from ancient hunting dogs to modern companions. Understanding these categories helps appreciate the diverse roles dogs play in our lives.

This site is created for fun and provides brief information about dog breeds. If you need more details, please visit the official American Kennel Club (AKC) website to find comprehensive information about your future pet.
